Roast Chicken Recipe

Description

This roast chicken is delicious and simple dish, perfect a family Sunday dinner. It goes perfectly with roast potatoes and vegetable. The leftovers are also great for making sandwiches and the carcass can be used to make a delicious chicken stock.

Ingredients

  • 1 (~1.5 kg) whole chicken, giblets removed
  • Zest from 1 lemon
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened to room temperature
  • 1 tablespoon rosemary leaves, roughly chopped
  • 2 medium onions, cut roughly in quarters

Steps

  1. Comine the lemon zest, minced garlic, rosemary and butter in a bowl.
  2. Using a clean hand or spatula, rub the mixture over the chicken and under the skin.
  3. Place the chicken on top of the onion quarters in a large roasting tray.
  4. Place the chicken in an oven preheated to 190°C and cook for 1 hour and 15 minutes or until the internal temperature at the thickest part of the thigh reads 65°C.
  5. Remove from the oven and let rest for 30 minutes.
